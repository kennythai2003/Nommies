# Nommies

Nommies is a social media platform for sharing food and restaurant experiences. Users will have their own unique profile where they will be able to post about their experiences and interact with other usersâ€™ posts. Users who do not create an account will also be able to browse a feed of posts, but cannot interact with others. Restaurants and food experiences will be sourced from Yelp using their own API. Administrators will have special permission to access insights of the website.
